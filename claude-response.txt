PHOTOPIA vs WISHBRINGER - Why Photopia stays in "Your First Adventure"

PHOTOPIA = "Watch a movie"
- You experience a story
- No friction, no puzzles
- Can't fail or get stuck
- Shows newcomers that IF is worth their time

WISHBRINGER = "Play a game"
- Real puzzles to solve
- Inventory management
- Can get stuck
- Teaches how to actually play IF

CONCLUSION:
Photopia stays in "Your First Adventure" - it hooks people on the medium.
Wishbringer is right at home in Classics - for when they're ready to learn puzzles.

FREE HOSTING OPTIONS WITH HTTPS
================================

STATIC SITES / FRONTEND
-----------------------

GitHub Pages
- Free HTTPS with username.github.io subdomain
- Custom domain support (HTTPS included)
- Just push to a repo
- https://pages.github.com

Netlify
- Free tier with yoursite.netlify.app subdomain
- Automatic HTTPS
- Drag-and-drop deploy or Git integration
- https://netlify.com

Vercel
- Free tier with yoursite.vercel.app
- Automatic HTTPS
- Great for React, Vue, static sites
- https://vercel.com

Cloudflare Pages
- Free tier with yoursite.pages.dev
- Unlimited bandwidth on free tier
- Fast CDN-backed hosting
- https://pages.cloudflare.com


FULL STACK (WITH BACKEND)
-------------------------

Render
- Node.js, Python, etc.
- Free tier with yoursite.onrender.com
- Spins down after inactivity (cold starts)
- https://render.com

Railway
- Easy deployment
- $5 free credit/month
- yoursite.up.railway.app
- https://railway.app

Glitch
- Good for quick experiments
- Free with yoursite.glitch.me
- Live editing in browser
- https://glitch.com


QUICK RECOMMENDATION
--------------------

For IFTalk-style testing, Netlify or Vercel are best:
- Drag-and-drop your public/ folder
- Instant HTTPS
- No credit card required
- Takes about 2 minutes
